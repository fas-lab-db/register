What is Fasfood?

Fasfood is a service that helps small food shops, specifically Fastfood, Restaurants and Chisanyama, sell their food to more customers through a platform where people can order from them.

The shop owner joins the platform and lists the food they sell. Customers can then discover local shops and order food online for pickup or delivery.

For Shop Owners

  • Monthly fee: Based on how many food items the shop wants to display (about R15 per menu item).
  • Example: 10 items would cost around R150 per month.
  • Optional till system: Helps manage orders and track sales for about R50 per month.
  • Adverts: Shops can create adverts from R15/day.
  • Extra addons: 3 Extra addons free and from 4 is R10 each
Bonus: First 3 add-ons included at no cost. Additional add-ons (4+) only R10 each.

For Customers

  • Discover local food shops near you
  • Browse menus and prices
  • Order online for pickup or delivery
  • Support small businesses in your community
Shops grow visibility with smart add-ons & daily ads.

Add‑on summary: 3 add‑ons free, from the 4th add‑on → R10 each (ideal for extra features, menu highlights, priority placement).

Combine with adverts to maximize daily reach.

In simple terms, the shop pays a small monthly fee to be listed on the platform so customers can discover them and order food from them.
